Rules of the ping pong game


You will enter a positive whole number into the text box below and the program will output all numbers from 1 to your inputted number.


With a few exeptions listed below

  • Numbers divisable by 3 display as "ping"
  • Numbers divisable by 5 display as "pong"
  • Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 display as "pingpong"
